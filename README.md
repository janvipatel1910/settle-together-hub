# Settle Together Hub

## Mission
Settle Together Hub is a community-driven support platform designed to help international students and newcomers settle safely and affordably in the UK.

The platform aims to provide guidance for accommodation, food support, job assistance, and emotional/community support for people who arrive without strong local connections.

## Vision
To build a trusted technology-enabled support ecosystem that helps newcomers feel safe, supported, and connected while building their future in the UK.

## Future Features
- Accommodation support
- Roommate matching
- Food/mess support
- Job guidance
- AI student assistant
- Community groups
- Emergency support system
- Student onboarding tools
